Drive to Own

Turn your driving hustle into ownership

Peneh Credit Investment and Services Limited offers a "drive to own" service, a unique financing solution designed to empower drivers in Zambia. This scheme allows individuals to acquire a vehicle by operating it for commercial purposes (e.g., as a taxi or Yango) while making regular payments.

The core benefit is that upon successful completion of the contract terms, including all agreed-upon payments, the driver takes full ownership of the vehicle, transforming their earning capacity into an asset.

This model provides an accessible pathway to vehicle ownership, especially for drivers in the transport sector, by aligning income generation directly with asset acquisition.
